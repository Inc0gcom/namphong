Hello There

We Are Namphong Racing

Nam Phong means Southern Wind in Vietnamese. Since wind is the vigorous force of nature and effortlessly fast, it signifies the speed of our car. Moreover, through our aerodynamic engineering, we aim to seamlessly overcome drag. In this way, we are in a constant "fight" with the wind; yet this is exactly where we grow and learn the most We proudly represent Vietnam through the image of a water buffalo logo, which is Vietnam's national animal. This not only reflects the honor of our culture heritage but it also emphasizes the sense of togetherness within our team
